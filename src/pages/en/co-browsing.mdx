Bring the speed and directness of in-person support and onboarding to your digital platform with our co-browsing feature in OpenReplay.


<Aside type="tip" title="Who can use this feature?">
    - Available on <a href="https://openreplay.com/pricing.html" class="text-blue-700 underline"><strong>all plans</strong></a>.
    - Can be customized according to <a href="https://docs.openreplay.com/en/deployment/roles-and-access/" class="text-blue-700 underline">defined roles and access</a> (Enterprise edition)
</Aside>


To use the Cobrowse feature, add the [**Assist plugin**](https://docs.openreplay.com/en/plugins/assist/) to your setup. Then, you can initiate co-browsing with any active user, enabling faster resolution of user support requests and a smoother onboarding experience.


### Engage in an active session

You can easily find specific online users who may need assistance on your site.

1. Go to the Cobrowse view.
2. Sort live sessions and apply any pre-defined metadata to find users.
3. Press the play button to monitor an online user's activity on your app.

</div>

<Aside type="tip" >
   You can monitor the activity of users navigating multiple tabs of your application
</Aside>

### Monitor console activities

You can monitor real-time user console activities and identify any arising errors, warnings, or information.

1. Play an online user session.
2. Click on the console at the bottom.

### Video call users

Hop on a video call to support or onboard clients while they navigate your application.

1. Play an online user session
2. Click on the call button and confirm your intention to call the user.

<div style={{ display: 'flex', justifyContent: 'center', alignItems: 'center' }}>
  <img src="/images/products/cobrowse/cobrowse_video_call.png" alt="cobrowse_video_call" style={{ width: '600px', height: 'auto' }} />
</div>

<Aside type="tip" >
   You can always choose to leave your camera off for an audio-only call.
</Aside>

### Record activity

If you need to record specific user activity for later review or onboarding, live sessions can be captured in video format.

1. Play an online user session
2. Click on the call button and confirm that you want to call the user.

<div style={{ display: 'flex', justifyContent: 'center', alignItems: 'center' }}>
  <img src="/images/products/cobrowse/cobrowse_record_call.png" alt="cobrowse_record_call" style={{ width: '600px', height: 'auto' }} />
</div>

### Remote control

To provide optimal support or onboarding for your client, you can guide them directly to their target using remote control.

1. Play an online user session
2. Click on the remote control and wait for the user's consent to grant remote access.
3. Once the user consents to remote control, you can use annotations to highlight items or draw directly on the user's screen.

### Multi-live sessions

1. Play an online user session
2. Click on the '+' button at the bottom to monitor up to four live sessions.
