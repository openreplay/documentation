**Similar Sessions** is an AI-powered feature in OpenReplay that allows users to find session replays similar to the one they are currently analyzing. It helps developers and product managers quickly identify patterns, recurring issues, and related user behaviors without manually searching.

<Aside type="caution" title="Availability" >
  - Limited to **Cloud (Serverless)** users at this time.
</Aside>

## How to Access Similar Sessions
1. Open a **Session Replay** in OpenReplay.
2. Click on the **Similar Sessions** button at the top.
3. The AI will generate a list of sessions that share common patterns with the current session.
4. Select a session to explore related interactions and behaviors.
