**Summary AI** is an AI-powered feature in OpenReplay that generates concise summaries of session replays. It helps users quickly understand the key events in a session without watching the entire replay.


<Aside type="ai" title="Availability" >
  - Limited to Cloud (Serverless) users at this time.
</Aside>


## How to Use Summary AI
1. Navigate to the **Session Replays** list view.
2. Click on a session to open its replay.
3. Click on the **Summary AI** button next to the DevTools section.
4. Review the AI-generated session summary to get a quick overview of user behavior and issues and errors.
