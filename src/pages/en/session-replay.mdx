Session Replay allows you to understand user behavior by reproducing their journey through your application. With OpenReplay, you can troubleshoot issues, analyze interactions, and improve your product with ease.

## Guides
- [Omnisearch](/session-replay/omnisearch): Quickly search across sessions with our powerful search feature.
- [Highlights](/session-replay/highlights): Isolate and save meaningful portions of a session replay

## Advanced Setup

- [Identify a User](/session-replay/identify-user): Track individual users across sessions with user identification.
- [Send Metadata](/session-replay/send-metadata): Attach custom metadata to enrich session insights.
- [Canvas and WebGL](/session-replay/canvas-webgl): Replay complex visual content, including Canvas and WebGL-based interactions.
- [Cross-domain iFrames](/session-replay/cross-domain-iframes): Enable cross-domain iFrame tracking.
- [WebSockets](/session-replay/websockets):  Record WebSocket channels and display them in the session replay.
- [Error Tracking](/session-replay/error-tracking): Identify and troubleshoot errors directly from session replays.
